Mouth watering Garlic Shrimp Recipe

How to make the best Garlic shrimp

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
    •  one bag of frozen shrimp
  • Onion
    •  a whole onion
  • Fresh garlic
    •  some nice garlic cloves
  • Spices and seasonings
    •  two cups of garlic powder and a pinch of ghost pepper

Directions

  1. Mince the garlic
    2.  cut garlic cloves into tiny pieces
  2. Cut up the onion
    3.  dice onion
  3. Sprinkle garlic on shrimp
    4.  dump garlic powder on the shrimp
  4. Cook the shrimp
    5.  put all the shrimp on a pan at half heat for 10 minutes
  5. Enjoy
