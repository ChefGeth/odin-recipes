Cheesey Mac and Cheese Recipe

How to make the best Garlic shrimp

Ingredients

  • Noodles
    •  one box of kd
  • Cheese
    •  a block of your favorite cheese
  • Fresh garlic
    •  some nice garlic cloves
  • Bacon
    •  one whole pack of bacon

Directions

  1. Cook the noodles
    2.  Boil water and put pasta in it
  2. Shread the cheese
    3.  Shread the cheese with a cheese grater
  3. Cook Bacon
    4.  Fry up some bacon while the pasta cooks
  4. Strain pasta
    5.  strain the noodles and put cheese on them
  5. Mix the bacon
    6.  cut up and stir in that bacon
